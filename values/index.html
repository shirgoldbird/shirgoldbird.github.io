Values

There are 3 core values I seek out in my working environments. I believe that environments that provide these are often lead to the best outcomes for teams and companies.

Collaboration: A workplace should reward free sharing of information and cross-functional teamwork.

Challenge: I want to work in a place where I'm regularly facing new, interesting problems.

Compassion: I want to work in an environment where treating coworkers with respect and kindess is the norm.
